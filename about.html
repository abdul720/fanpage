The goal of the game (based on the map) will be escort teh payload (a cart that will only move if your teammates are near it) also if both the team mate and the enemy are on the payload it will not be moved it will stay in one place.

On other maps you might need to capture and hold a point, or capture point and then escort the payload. Basiclly a capture point map has 2-3 max rounds based on which team wins the round. The way it works is the first team to capture and hold a point for 2 rounds wins the game. To capture the point the team is suppose to only have their teammates on the point if the enemy is also on the point the capture progress will not move for anyteam (blue and red) the maps that have the capture system like this are ilios, lijiang tower, and nepal.
